Tutor Disclosure Agreement

Purpose

This Tutor Disclosure Agreement governs the professional relationship between C&T Tutorials and registered tutors offering academic support services through the platform.

Professional Conduct

  • Tutors shall maintain academic integrity at all times.
  • Tutors shall not complete assignments on behalf of students.
  • Tutors shall respect confidentiality of all student information.
  • Tutors agree to adhere to C&T Tutorials policies and ethical standards.

Independent Contractor Status

Tutors operate as independent service providers and are not employees of C&T Tutorials.

Confidentiality

All academic materials, communications, and student information must remain confidential.
