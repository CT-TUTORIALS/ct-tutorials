About C&T Tutorials

C&T Tutorials is an independent academic support service committed to enhancing students’ academic performance through structured guidance in academic writing, research, and communication skills.

Our services provide focused and student-centred academic support through personalised tutorials, collaborative group learning, and assignment-based guidance.

Our Services

Academic Writing Tutorials

Structured sessions covering academic writing principles, essay development, and research techniques.

Group Learning Sessions

Collaborative sessions designed to support multiple students working on similar academic challenges.

Assignment Support

Professional assistance with editing, proofreading, and improving assignment structure and clarity.

Public Speaking Support

Coaching sessions designed to strengthen presentation and communication skills for academic purposes.
