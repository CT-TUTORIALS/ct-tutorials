About C&T Tutorials

C&T Tutorials is an independent academic support service committed to enhancing students’ academic performance through structured guidance in academic writing, research, and communication skills.

Our services provide focused and student-centred academic support through personalised tutorials, collaborative group learning, and assignment-based guidance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you write assignments for students?

No. We do not complete assignments on behalf of students. We provide guidance and support to help students improve their own work.

How are sessions conducted?

Sessions are conducted online or in person, depending on availability and student preference.

How does payment work?

Payment details are provided after registration and must be completed before sessions are confirmed.

Can I choose my tutor?

Yes, students may request a tutor based on availability.
