Frequently Asked Questions

Do you write assignments for students?

No. We do not complete assignments on behalf of students. Our role is to guide, support, and help students improve their academic work while maintaining academic integrity.

How are sessions conducted?

Sessions are conducted either online or in person, depending on availability and student preference.

How does payment work?

Payment details and instructions are provided after registration. All sessions must be confirmed before they take place.

Can I choose my tutor?

Yes. Where possible, students may request a specific tutor based on availability.
