About C&T Tutorials

C&T Tutorials is an independent academic support service committed to enhancing students’ academic performance through structured guidance in academic writing, research, and communication skills. The organisation prioritises clarity, coherence, and academic integrity across all areas of student engagement.

Our services are designed to provide focused, student-centred support through one-on-one tutorials, group sessions, and assignment-based assistance. Emphasis is placed on developing critical thinking, effective argumentation, and adherence to accepted academic conventions.

Independence Disclaimer

C&T Tutorials operates independently and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or formally connected to any university, college, examination body, or academic institution. All services provided are supplementary in nature.

C&T Tutorials does not complete assignments, examinations, or assessments on behalf of students. Responsibility for all submitted academic work remains entirely with the student.
